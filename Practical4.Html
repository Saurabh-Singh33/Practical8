NAAC A+ accredited multidisciplinary university on the Rajkot–Morbi highway, empowering learners with industry-ready skills and strong research culture.

Vision

To be a leading university that fosters innovation, research, and holistic development, producing socially responsible professionals and entrepreneurs.

Mission

  • Deliver outcome-based education aligned with global standards.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research, incubation and start-up culture.
  • Collaborate with industry for experiential learning and placements.
  • Build an inclusive, student-centric campus ecosystem.

Program Specific Outcomes (CSE)

PSO-1: Software Design & Development

Apply core CS fundamentals to analyze, design, implement and test robust software systems using modern tools, frameworks, and best practices.

PSO-2: Data & Intelligence

Model, process and interpret data to build intelligent solutions leveraging databases, machine learning, and cloud-native pipelines.

PSO-3: Professional Practice

Demonstrate teamwork, ethics, effective communication, and continuous learning to deliver technology solutions with societal impact.
